# A.08 Risk Management Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy formally defines the risk management methodology for FusionEMS to identify, evaluate, treat, and monitor information security risks continuously.

## 2. Risk Assessment Methodology
1. **Annual Assessments:** A formal, documented Information Security Risk Assessment shall be conducted at least annually, or upon any significant system/architectural change.
2. **Identification:** Assets, threats, vulnerabilities, and likelihoods will be mapped.
3. **Scoring:** Risks are scored as a product of Likelihood x Impact (Low, Medium, High, Critical).

## 3. Risk Treatment Procedures
Any risk scoring 'High' or 'Critical' MUST have a formally documented treatment plan assigned to an owner within 15 days of identification.
Available Treatments:
- **Mitigate / Remediate:** Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact (preferred).
- **Transfer:** Shift financial/operational burden via insurance or third-party SLA.
- **Accept:** Formal management sign-off is explicitly required to accept any medium/high risks. Critical risks CANNOT be accepted without CEO/CISO dual signatory.
- **Avoid:** Cease the activity causing the risk.

## 4. Framework Mappings
- **ISO 27001:** Clause 6.1 (Actions to address risks and opportunities), 8.2 (Information security risk assessment)
- **SOC 2:** CC3.1, CC3.2
- **HIPAA:** 164.308(a)(1)(ii)(A) (Risk Analysis)
