# Security Awareness and Training Program

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Policy ID | SAT-001 |
| Version | 1.0 |
| Effective Date | March 9, 2026 |
| Owner | Security Officer |

## Program Scope

Applies to all employees and contractors with FusionEMS access.

## Training Cadence

- Onboarding: within 14 days
- Annual refresher: required for all staff
- Role-specific modules: engineers, PHI handlers, support staff
- Incident-triggered retraining: required after material violations/incidents

## Required Topics

- Phishing and business email compromise
- Credential hygiene and MFA use
- PHI handling and HIPAA minimum necessary rule
- Secure coding (OWASP + dependency hygiene)
- Incident reporting and escalation pathways
- Vendor/social engineering risks

## Delivery Model

- LMS-based baseline modules
- Quarterly live workshops
- Monthly phishing simulations

## Completion Standards

- Minimum quiz score: 80%
- Missed deadline escalation to manager + Security Officer
- Access restriction may apply for repeat non-compliance

## Evidence

- Training completion logs
- Quiz score exports
- Phishing simulation reports
- Remediation/training follow-up records
