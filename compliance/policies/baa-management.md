# BAA Management Procedure

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Policy ID | BAA-001 |
| Version | 1.0 |
| Effective Date | March 9, 2026 |
| Owner | Security Officer |

## Requirement

A signed Business Associate Agreement is mandatory before any vendor/subprocessor can access or potentially access PHI.

## Minimum BAA Terms

- Permitted uses and disclosures
- Safeguards and HIPAA obligations
- Subcontractor flow-down obligations
- Breach notification obligations/timelines
- Return/destruction at termination

## Workflow

1. Vendor identified and PHI risk classified
2. Legal review of BAA terms
3. Security Officer approval
4. Executive signature
5. Register update before production activation

## BAA Register Fields

- Vendor
- PHI scope
- Execution date
- Renewal/expiration date
- Breach notice contact
- Last review date

## Breach Chain

Subprocessor -> FusionEMS -> Covered Entity -> Individuals/HHS per legal obligations.

## Evidence

- Executed BAAs
- BAA register
- Annual review records
