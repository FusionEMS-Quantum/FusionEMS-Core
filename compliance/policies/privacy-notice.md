## What We Collect

- Demographics and identity data
- Medical and encounter records (ePCR, vitals, CAD context)
- Insurance and billing-related data
- Operational/account security data

## How We Use Data

- Treatment, payment, and healthcare operations (HIPAA TPO)
- Platform operations, security, reliability, compliance
- Limited communications and support operations

## Sharing

- Covered entities/agencies under contract
- Business associates/subprocessors under BAA/DPA controls
- Regulators/law enforcement where legally required

## Security Measures

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Least-privilege access with MFA
- Tenant isolation and audit trails
- Continuous monitoring and incident response controls

## Patient/User Rights

- Access and copies of records
- Amendment/correction requests
- Restriction and confidential communication requests
- Accounting of disclosures
- Complaint rights

## Retention

Data is retained per policy and legal requirements, including 7-year PHI retention baseline unless stricter law/contract applies.
